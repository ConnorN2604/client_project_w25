At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we are committed to supporting our students' academic success by offering a wide array of resources and services.

Our academic advising team provides personalized guidance to help you navigate your curriculum, select courses, and address any academic challenges or questions you may encounter during your studies.

UMSI students also have access to the extensive collections and study spaces within the University Library system, offering both digital and physical resources essential for research and coursework.

Additionally, our specialized tutoring services and the UMSI Writing Center are available to support you in mastering complex subjects and enhancing your written communication skills.

By utilizing these academic resources, UMSI students can thrive in their studies and achieve their educational goals.

How Can Your Advisor Help You?

  • Creating an individualized academic plan
  • Interpreting an academic policy or procedure
  • Discussing concerns about a particular course
  • Connecting with student support resources
  • Navigating a disruption to your BSI experience

Which Service is Right for me?

  • Scheduled 1:1 Appointments are 15 or 30-minute appointments and must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance of the meeting time. These meetings are often the best option when you have a unique or complex question or concern. In some cases, your advisor may ask, suggest, or require you to schedule an appointment if you send an email or come to drop-in 1:1 advising with a question that requires additional time
  • Drop-in 1:1 Advising Appointments are offered weekly and do not require pre-registration. Students may be seen by any member of the advising team during a drop-in appointment and are seen on a first-come, first-served basis. Drop-ins are a great option if you have a time-sensitive question that can be addressed in under 10 minutes.
  • Email is a great way to connect with our advising team asynchronously, which can be helpful for students managing multiple responsibilities. Email also provides you with a written record of our conversation that you can reference later. Please note that we aim to respond to student emails within 24 business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM, Monday through Friday excluding holidays), but response times may vary.
